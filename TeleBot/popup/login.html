TelePort-How to?

1. Go to web.telegram.com on your mobile.

2. Sign up using your phone number.

3. Search for "@ReceiveFromEdge" and click on Receive From Edge

4. Send /start to begin chatting.

5. Wait for a 4 digit PIN and enter in your extension.

6. You are good to go. Start sending your things.
